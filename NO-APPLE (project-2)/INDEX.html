Why someone would build their own PC?

People choose to build their own PC for a variety of compelling reasons, often seeking advantages that off-the-shelf pre-built systems simply can't offer. Here's a breakdown of the main motivations:

Get Exactly What You Need: This is arguably the biggest reason. When you build a PC, you handpick every single component ‚Äì the CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, motherboard, power supply, case, and cooling. This allows you to create a machine optimized precisely for your specific needs.

For Gamers: You can prioritize a high-end graphics card and fast RAM for smooth, high-resolution gameplay, without overpaying for a powerful CPU if your games aren't CPU-intensive.

For Content Creators (Video Editing, 3D Rendering): You can focus on a powerful multi-core CPU and ample, fast RAM, along with sufficient storage for large files.
